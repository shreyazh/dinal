Photography by Zyair

I, Dinal Sharma, go by the stage name Zyair, a visual storyteller and poet at heart. Through the lens and the lines, I capture emotion, movement, and meaning.
My work spans photography, videography, and cinematography, blending technical precision with artistic vision. Whether it's a fleeting glance, a raw landscape, or a story told in slow motion, I strive to make every frame feel alive.
Alongside visuals, I write poetry that echoes the same depth, soft, sharp, and unapologetically human. Words and visuals are just different languages I speak to explore what it means to feel.
Here, you'll find moments frozen, emotions filmed, and thoughts spilled. Welcome to my world.
